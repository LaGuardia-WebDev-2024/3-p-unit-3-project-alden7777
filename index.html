The Very Best Steak And Potatos You'll Ever Eat. Ever.

  • Time: 45 miniutes, ish
  • Serves: 2-3

Contents:

  1. Steak
  2. Potatos

The Steak

Ingredients Quantity
Steak (Any Cut) 2
Butter 1/2 tbsp
Salt to taste
Thyme 3 springs
Pepper to taste
Garlic 2 cloves

The Potatos

Ingredients Quantity
Baby Potatos 15-20
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Salt to taste
peprika 1/2 tsp
Pepper to taste

Step 1: Preheat oven to 425F. Boil pot of water.

Step 2: Add whole baby potatos to boiling water, boil for 10-15 miniutes, or until soft.

Step 3: Remove potatos from pot, dry, then arrange on olive oil-drizzled sheetpan.

Step 4: Smash potatos using broad side of a large knife, then drizzle with more olive oil and seasonings. Put in top shelf of oven for 5-10 miniutes, or until golden brown and crispy.

Step 5: After placing potatos in oven, preheat pan on high heat. Smash garlic cloves and add to pan with butter.

Step 6: Add steaks to pan, sprinkle salt, pepper, add whole sprigs of thyme. sear for 2-3 miniutes on each side, depending on thickness of cut.

Step 7: Turn heat down on pan, cook until desired temperature has been reached.

Step 7: Serve steaks with smashed potatos. Enjoy thoroughly.
