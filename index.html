The Steak

Ingredients Quantity
Steak (Any Cut) 2
Butter 1/2 teaspoon
Salt to taste
Thyme 3 springs
Pepper to taste
Garlic 2 cloves

Step 1: Begin heating pan on high.

Step 2: Peel then smash garlic cloves with broad side of knife. Add to pan with butter.

Step 3: Add steaks to pan, sprinkle salt and pepper. Add whole thyme sprigs.
